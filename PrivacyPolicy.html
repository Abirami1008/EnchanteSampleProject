SHIPPING POLICY

  1. Shipping is free within India for bill value above Rs.2000
  2. We ship both Domestic and International.
  3. Once your order is placed and confirmed, the product will be despatched maximum within 48 hours.
  4. Any customization like saree falls, blouses etc will take additional time 7 to 15 working days depending on the level of customization.
  5. We can only process one shipping address per order. So, if you would like to order several items and ship to different people or locations, please make these as separate orders.
  6. We cannot ship to Post Office Box addresses and take no responsibility for non delivery of orders in case the delivery address includes P. O. Box details in any part or form.
  7. Our shipping service will attempt delivery 2 times at the delivery address specified by you in the order, before they contact us that your package is not deliverable. To further avoid these issues, customers are requested to share only addresses where they are available within working hours to accept the package.
